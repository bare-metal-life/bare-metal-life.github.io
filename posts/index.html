God and Religion

God and/versus Religion There is only one definition of the word “God” that makes sense to me: That which caused the existence of the cosmos, including me. I exist The Cosmos exists Something caused the Cosmos and me to exist That Something gets the name “God” This concept of God radically simplifies this thing we call “Religion”. It is simply about connecting with the Creator. The Cosmos could be self-creating but to my mind this is absurd.

Tepache

Six times round the square The call to prayer from the nearby mosque wakes us up early most mornings. If we don’t go out in time, it gets too hot to walk. 8am is the limit. We walk through the Muslim quarter where all the breakfast places are already full with guys filling their tummies before they go off to work. Each one has his bird in its cage. The bird is a member of the family and will be taken to one of the regular birdsong competitions in the town.
